Get to Know Our Resort

Emerald Resort has been a cornerstone in Porto Circle, Mariveles, Bataan, as the first and only resort in the area. Since its inception, the resort has steadily expanded, contributing significantly to the growth of Porto Circle's business and community. Driven by the goal to provide equal, high-quality service to all visitors, Emerald Resort ensures an exceptional experience for guests from all walks of life. The resort features multiple swimming pools, a bar with a variety of refreshing drinks, and electric-powered cottages for comfortable day use. For longer stays, cozy cabins are available, offering a relaxing retreat or a convenient stopover for travelers heading to Panoypoy, the resort’s serene backyard cove beach. At Emerald Resort, we strive to create a welcoming and unforgettable escape for everyone.

Our Vision

To be the top destination in Mariveles Bataan,offering unmatched hospitality, celebrating diversity, and creating a welcoming space where everyone feels connected and valued.

Our Mision

To provide an inclusive and welcoming sanctuary for all guests, where relaxation,enjoyment, and memorable experiences are accessible to everyone.
